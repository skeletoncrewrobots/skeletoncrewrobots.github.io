2019 in Summary

    In 2019 we performed far better than our rookie year, at our first event we wound up as the captian for the 5th alliance, and finished as quarterinalists. At our second competiton we got much farther, we were picked by the first place alliance and went all the way to the finals and achieved second place. Due to our performance during he season we qualified for the North Carolina State Championship and finished 30th there. All in all we learned an incredible amount this year, and are inspired to make it back next year!
